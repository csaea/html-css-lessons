Welcome to the Year 2007

It's the Wild West of the internet. Less regulated, less commercialized, less everything.

But you no longer need a phoneline. We have broadband!

Selfies are not a thing.

We are nearing the end of the "static page" era of the internet and entering the "real-time web".

But sites are still page based, built on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. There are no apps online.

Smartphones are in their infancy. There are no apps, no mobile sites. It's just the desktop web in a tiny screen.

The concept of getting "Followers" on social media did not exist. You could have 5,000 friends max on Facebook, or 8,000 on MySpace.

YouTube is a global platform for casually uploading personal videos. There are no ads. The max resolution is 360p.

Internet in 2007

  • Chat: AOL chat rooms and MSN Instange Messenge.
  • Maps: Search a location on Google Maps then print it.
  • Email: AOL, Hotmail, ICQ
